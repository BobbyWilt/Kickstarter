Predict View Plots
Kickstarter



This project takes in kickstarter data and seeks to predict if a kickerstarter campgain will be successful or not.
Pressing the predict button on the top right will generate a few predictions for several kickerstarter campaigns.
These predictions will either be that the kickstarter was successful or failed.
Clicking on the "View Plots" button will display view descriptive plots for our model that we built to predict Kickstarter success.
